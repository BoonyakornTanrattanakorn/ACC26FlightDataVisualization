About this dashboard

A static, dependency-free dashboard for the Air Cargo Challenge 2026 flight telemetry. Browse the full competition standings, then dive into any team's flight to see its GPS ground track, altitude, battery voltage and current draw over time. Data is served straight from the recorded flights — every raw sample is kept (lossless).

🏆 Leaderboard

Full competition standings — flight, presentation, drawings, report and penalties. Sort by any column and jump straight to a team's flight.
